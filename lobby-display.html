Hebrew School Spotlight

Bringing Home The Gold

Our Hebrew School champion represented Chabad of Minneapolis at the Grand International Jew-Q Championship in New York — and brought home the gold medal.

A testament to the dedication of our students, teachers, and the entire Hebrew School program.

Jew-Q is a global educational competition for Jewish children that challenges them to master fundamental concepts of Jewish history, laws, and traditions.
